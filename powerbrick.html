The Powerbrick

Your friendly power source.



The Loshollian PowerBrick is similar to the other powerbanks, as it uses a standard port outlets to power phones. Where it differs is its form factor. The Loshollian PowerBrick crams a respectable 25,000 mAh battery into a fairly small package. Measuring 6.5″ x 2.5″ x 2.5″ and weighing only 1.5 lbs, it’s one of the smallest and lightest laptop power banks on the market. However, just because it’s small doesn’t mean it doesn’t pack a punch. The PowerBrick is capable of delivering a whopping 85W of power. In addition to the AC power outlet, the PowerBrick also features three USB charging ports and is solar powered.



Specifications

Capacity: 25,000mAh

Input: USB Type-C (9V/2A), Micro-USB (9V/2A) & Lightning (5V/2.1A)

Output: USB Type-C (12V/1.5A, 9V/2A & 5V/3A) & 2 x USB (12V/1.5A, 9V/2A, 5V/3A & 5V/2.1A)

Battery type: Lithium ion

Solar Panel: Arduino Compatible 5.0V 200mA Solar Cell

Charging time: 10-11 hours (5V/2A ac adapter)

Size: 6.5″ x 2.5″ x 2.5″

Weight: 680 g or 150 lbs
