This PowerBrick is similar with the other power banks the market has; however, this power source is solar powered and has an AC outlet. It's either you plug-in the adapter or the usb.



It can charge your phone, laptop, drone, camera battery or any devices with battery not bigger than a laptop.



It can last for a day (minimum) until three days (maximum).



Devices such as TV are not supported by our PowerBrick.



The power bank has 25,000 mAH



It last depending on how you use it. It can charge your phone 8 times which could last atleast 3-5 days. With your laptops, it can only last through 1 usage: while in use - up to 50%, not in use - up to 70%
